Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 2 Eggs
  • 50g Parmesan Cheese
  • 100g Bacon
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Fry bacon until crispy.
  3. Mix eggs and cheese in a bowl.
  4. Combine everything and season.
